is ANB Proof of stake or Proof of work?

ANB is advance POS (Proof-of-Stake); a scheme built on less-costly computations. This implies that the proof of stake scheme is not based on costly computations as compared to the proof of work scheme. Rather than depending on the scarce resources (costly computations), the proof of stake scheme is dependent on the ACCESS FOR ALL: NEXT BLOCKCHAIN OF THE FUTURE www.anbcoin.info entities that hold stake within the network (this implies proof of stake holding).
